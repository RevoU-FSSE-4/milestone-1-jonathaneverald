Who we are

We have 15 years of experience

For 15 years, BSU Garage has been a staple in the community, providing drivers with exceptional car repair and maintenance services. We understand the importance of a reliable vehicle, and our passionate team is dedicated to keeping your car running smoothly and safely for miles to come.


Our team members are not just experienced, they are ASE-certified technicians who stay up-to-date on the latest automotive advancements, ensuring they can diagnose and repair any issue your vehicle may encounter. We are committed to transparency throughout the entire process, providing you with clear explanations and upfront estimates before any work begins. When you choose BSU Garage, you can rest assured that we use only the highest-quality tools and genuine car parts, ensuring a flawless and long-lasting repair. We understand budgets matter, and that's why we offer competitive prices and flexible payment options to fit your needs.


But BSU Garage goes beyond just repairs. We are your trusted advisor, offering preventative maintenance services to keep your car running optimally and avoiding costly repairs down the road. We can help with routine oil changes, tune-ups, tire rotations, and brake inspections, ensuring your vehicle operates at peak performance and minimizes future issues. Whether you need a routine oil change or a major repair, BSU Garage is here for you. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service, building trust, and ensuring your complete satisfaction. Visit us today or schedule an appointment online to experience the BSU Garage difference!
